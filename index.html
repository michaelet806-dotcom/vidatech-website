06 — The Honest Questions

Before you book a call.

Will the receptionist sound robotic?+

Listen yourself — call the demo line in the audit. It greets the way you'd greet, handles the same objections, and hands off to a human if anyone asks for one. We don't ship anything you wouldn't be proud to answer with.

What if it gets a call wrong?+

Every transcript lands in your inbox. We listen too. Anything that needed to escalate gets routed to your phone within minutes. We tune the system weekly — your edge cases get fewer, never more.

How is this not just another chatbot?+

Chatbots wait for someone to click a widget. Our receptionist picks up your phone. It speaks. It books on the calendar you already use. And it's accountable to the same metric a human receptionist is — booked appointments, not "interactions."

Is my industry supported?+

Dental, auto repair, HVAC, law, real estate, salons, restaurants, contractors. If you're a service business and you take phone calls, you're supported. The audit is industry-specific — no generic templates.

What if I want out?+

30-day notice, no contract, no questions. We keep your number, your data, your call history portable. If we're not earning our keep, we shouldn't be there.

Tried automation before and it failed. Why try again?+

Most "automation" is one tool bolted on, run by you. We operate the whole loop — and we're the ones the alerts go to at 2am. The difference is who's accountable when something breaks.
